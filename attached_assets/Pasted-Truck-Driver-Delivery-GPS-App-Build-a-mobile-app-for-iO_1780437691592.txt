Truck Driver Delivery GPS App
Build a mobile app for iOS and Android called EverStop
App Purpose
Everstop GPS is a community-driven GPS and delivery location database designed specifically for truck drivers, delivery drivers, carriers, and customers.
The goal is to help drivers find the exact place to park, unload, check in, and complete deliveries while saving time, reducing missed deliveries, and improving safety.
The app works similarly to a GPS app but uses information submitted by drivers and customers.
